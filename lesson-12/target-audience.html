Persona 2

  • Age: 25
  • Schooling: Associate's Degree in Communication
  • Social Status: Single, 2 Children

Visiting Purpose ( Website )

Being a successful professional is busy and requires keeping a healthy living and continual balance in a stressful workplace. Having to raise 2 Children alone requires providing the family with healthy food and nutrients,but not having too much time to go to the market.

I want my family to eat healthy fruit but I'm not finding the right quality products. Does this website have a catalog or some pictures of their products? Is there a way to buy the products online and receive them through a delivery service?

Scenarios

1) A Housewife trying to find the right products for her children at the right price.

2) A man trying to save more time and energy buying their food through an online store .

3) Someone looking for the fruits of the current season, to see what will be on the menu for the next week.

4) Small and medium-sized businesses looking for a complete distribution service and the right products

5) Tourists looking for tasting the typical fruits of the season of the place that they are going to visit in the next summer vacation.
