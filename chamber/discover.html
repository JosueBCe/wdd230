The Rosario Chamber of Commerce is one of the most recognized assocations in this field in Argentina. His mission is to:

  • Generate, promote and support strategic projects that contribute to the economic development of their region of belonging.
  • Provide Quality Analysis and Certification services on basic products, derivatives and inputs.
  • Promote and offer the business community an efficient arbitration regime as a means of dispute resolution.

The Chamber of Commerce of the Chaco is the most important in all of Northeast Argentina. His mission is to:

  • Represent, defend and promote the rights and interests of those represented in terms of an egalitarian participatory democracy.
  • Act as a consultation and collaboration institution for the diagnosis, preparation and evaluation of public policies applied to our sector.
  • Promote and defend local economic and social development, through solidarity, professionalization and the preparation of legislative projects.
